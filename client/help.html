🏪 How do I add a new shop?

Click the "Add New Shop" button on your dashboard or in the sidebar. Fill in the shop name and details, then click "Create Shop". You can add unlimited shops based on your plan.

📦 How do I add items to my shop?

Open your shop from the dashboard, then click "Add Item". Enter the item name, quantity, price, and set a low stock alert threshold. Click "Save Item" to add it.

⚠️ How do low stock alerts work?

Set a minimum quantity threshold for each item. When stock falls below this number, you'll see an alert on your dashboard and in the shop view.

💳 How do I upgrade my plan?

Go to the Billing page from the sidebar, choose your desired plan (Premium or Enterprise), and click "Upgrade". Your new features will be available immediately.

📥 Can I export my data?

Yes! Premium and Enterprise users can export shop data as CSV files. Look for the "Export" button on your dashboard or shop pages.

🔒 Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use Firebase's enterprise-grade security with AES-256 encryption, secure authentication, and regular backups to protect your business data.

👥 Can I have multiple users?

Enterprise plan users can add team members with different permission levels (Owner, Manager, Staff). Contact support to enable team management.

📱 Is there a mobile app?

Currently, StockSmart Hub is web-based and works on all devices through your browser. A dedicated mobile app is coming soon!
